\begin{center}{\bf Math 215A --- UCB, Spring 2017 --- William H. Guss \\
Smooth Manifolds Examples --- Homework 2}
\\
\end{center}
We will revisit projctive spaces on a finite dimensional field $\mathbb{K}$ over $\mathbb{R}$ in addition to different maximal atlases on manifolds. For this assignment we will let $(X, A)$ be a smooth manifold with $X$ a topological manifold and $A$ a smooth structure on $X$.

\medskip \noindent {\bf (2.1a)}\ Consider the smooth manifold $X = \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{R}^2.$ Show that the one-point compactification of $X$ is homeomorphic to the Mobius band\footnote{Some authors refer to this as the Mobius strip. A good place to start is the quotient definition.}

\medskip \noindent {\bf (2.2b)}\ Let $\mathbb{X} = \mathbb{R}$. Give examples of multiple different smooth structures $A_i$ on $X.$
